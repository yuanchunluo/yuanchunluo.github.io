CV (updated July 2018)

Yuan-Chun Luo is currently a research assistant in electronic engineering at NCTU, where he is advised by Prof. Steve S. Chung, Chair Professor. His research interests are in emerging semiconductor devices, such as memory and FET. He also enjoys using software to analyze data from measurement and deal with physics-related problems.

In 2018, Yuan-Chun Luo received his B.S. degree from Electrical Engineering of National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) , Hsinchu, Taiwan.. In that year, he won the 2nd place award in the project contest held by the college of EECS with more than 250 senior undergraduate in EECS, NTHU. He was also the president of student association in EE, NTHU in 2016-2017
