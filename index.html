I am a Research assistant in electronic engineering at NCTU, where I am advised by Prof. Steve S. Chung, Chair Professor. My research interests are in emerging semiconductor devices, such as memory and FET.

In 2018, I graduated from NTHU with a bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering. In that year, I won the 2nd place award in the project contest held by the college of EECS with more than 250 senior undergraduate in EECS, NTHU. I was also the president of student association in EE, NTHU in 2016-2017
